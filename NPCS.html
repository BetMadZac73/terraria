About NPCs
NPCs are non Player characters that can effect anything from, mob spawn rates to health regeneration rates and this makes them quite useful to have around.
Most NPCs sell a variety of items and some different ones depending on the condition. Your first NPC (the guide) does not sell any items, but can tell you how to make all of the items ever available, unless obtained in another form of playing, such as defeating bosses or things found in a chest.
All NPCs have a certain form of self defense, even if it is not very effective (even at the start of the game).
