Night Time
Oh yes, when night falls and you are new to the game, you are going to want to keep yourself safe from Zombies and other nighly creatures. So it's best to close yourself into a Home or Cave on your first night, you will need torches and some wood to do this quickly. Do not worry too much about a door, just block yourself in and you can cut your way out in the morning.
However, night time need not be wasted, you can mine quite safely as long as you keep yourself blocked off from the Zombies. And as you collect new materials you will be able to craft your first items.

Torches require wood and Gel, you collect the Gel each time you defeat a slime (easiest in the day time, and the guide will kill some for you)
Tools you ar ebest to collect materials like metal ore (which you need to smelt in a furnace) and then make metal tools. Note you can also make pretty decent tools out of Cactus if you fin dyou are close to the desert.
Weapons are fashioned in a similar way to tools and the Cactus Sword is highly recommended as a much more effective weapon that the copper short sword that you got in your starter pack.
Armour is pretty importnat too!!! if you like to stay alive for longer then craft some armour out of available materials and put it on. You will find as you go through the game that items made from better materials give more protection, and some complete sets give a whole set Buff.
Health is always running down when we get attacked, injured, poisened, or fall from too great a height. Keep healing potions that you find in Vases and Chests handy, and press the 'H' key to heal.
Chests are all around the world, right-click a chest to open it and see what is inside, Loot it and then mine the Chest to take back to your base for storing allm your treasure and materials, because whilst a 40 slot inventory seems large, it soon gets full.
Vases are plentiful and all over the surface and underground, smash them with Axe or Sword and collect the goodies from inside, these include Rope, Torches, Coins, Bombs, some weapons and Ammunition. They are a quick way to collect immediately needed materials.
Acorns you are collecting a lot of wood, chopping down a lot of treas, but please be responsible and replant the acorns, this will prevent the world becoming barren and provide plenty more wood when you need it latter.
Mining by far the fastest way to progress in Terraria is to avoid spending pointlee hours/days mining, but it is good fun, so I advise run across the surface and access the underground where possible via existing entrances and caves, and mine when you need to in order to make progress, collect materials and to avoid dangerous mobs
