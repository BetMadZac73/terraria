Getting Started
The first thing you will need to do is collect some wood, use the Copper Axe that is item 3 in your hotbar, and chop down a tree.
collect the wood, and then you are going to be ready to do some crafting.
Press escape and then craft yourself a WorkBench.
Place down the workbench and then craft the required items
